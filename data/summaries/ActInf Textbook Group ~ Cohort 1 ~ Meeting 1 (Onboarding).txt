The onboarding session for the Active Inference textbook group covers the use of the collaborative CODA platform for learning. Participants are encouraged to engage in various activities and practices to enhance their learning experience. The group emphasizes open and authentic participation and encourages collaboration and communication. The textbook is a summary of ongoing research, and participants are encouraged to ask questions and engage in discussions. The speaker discusses the use of code in the book and the importance of integrity across different representations of active inference. The group offers various resources and opportunities for participation in the active inference research community.
